About Me

I am a young and upcoming Maltese photographer, ready to capture your special moments. I come from the background of a BSc in Creative Computing, where I gained expertise in both creative works but also technological know-how. Photography has been a great passion of mine for over 5 years now. I believe in building solid relationships with my clients, to create a fun and relaxing atmosphere. From events to portraits to commerical work, I will bring your photos to life.
